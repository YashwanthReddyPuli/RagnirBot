# ⚡ Introducing RagnirBot: The Ultimate LTC-Powered Gated Marketplace Bot ⚡

Are you tired of manually managing seller channels, tracking expirations, and dealing with marketplace scammers? 

Say hello to **RagnirBot**—the most advanced, self-operating Discord marketplace companion ever designed. It automates your entire marketplace structure using fast, low-fee **Litecoin (LTC)** payments, turning your server into a fully automated digital storefront.

---

## 🔮 What Makes RagnirBot Different? (The Feature Stack)

### 🤝 1. Automated LTC Escrow (Middleman) System
No more exit scams. RagnirBot acts as the ultimate automated escrow provider.
*   **How it works:** Buyers pay the seller directly through a bot-generated LTC address. The bot holds the funds in escrow. 
*   **Release Gating:** Once the buyer confirms delivery, the funds are instantly released to the seller's wallet. In case of disputes, staff can step in to review the chat and release the funds manually.

### ⚡ 2. Zero-Confirmation Mempool Gating (Instant Setup)
Nobody wants to wait for blockchain confirmations. 
*   **Instant Provisioning:** The moment a user sends the LTC payment, RagnirBot detects it in the mempool (0 confirmations) and instantly provisions their custom seller channel.
*   **Fail-Safe:** If the transaction does not confirm within 1 hour, the channel is automatically locked.

### 🎨 3. Visual Drag-and-Drop Embed Builder
Give your sellers the tools to stand out. 
*   Sellers can customize and edit their pinned channel banner, logo, description, and accent hex colors in real-time using interactive Discord Modals—no command lines required.

### 📈 4. Live Shop Analytics & CTR Tracking
RagnirBot tracks performance metrics for your sellers.
*   Every week, sellers receive a DM containing their channel's total views, peak active hours, and Click-Through Rate (CTR) on their custom buttons (like "Website" or "Buy Now").

### 📢 5. Gated Free vs. Premium Categories
*   **Free Tiers:** Automatically provisioned under a restricted category with shorter timers and read-only fallback states.
*   **Premium Tiers:** Featured in high-visibility categories, equipped with custom mention credits (e.g. 1 @everyone and 2 @here pings per slot duration) managed directly via `/slot ping` buttons.
